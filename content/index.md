# The Life Build Book

Welcome to The Life Build Book - a hypertext garden for exploring ideas, building knowledge, and designing a meaningful life.

This is your starting point. Add your thoughts, connect your ideas, and watch your knowledge grow.

## Getting Started

- Create new notes in your Obsidian vault
- Use `[[wikilinks]]` to connect ideas
- Organize with tags and folders
- Push to GitHub to publish
