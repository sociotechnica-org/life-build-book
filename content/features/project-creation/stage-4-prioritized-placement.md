---
title: "Stage 4: Prioritized Placement"
---

Stage 4 Prioritized Placement shows current Priority Queue and prompts directors to drag new project into position, determining where it ranks relative to existing priorities.

Directors consider urgency, importance, strategic alignment, and appropriate stream (Gold/Silver/Bronze based on archetype and scale). Normal pace takes 5 minutes; emergency pace takes 2 minutes with option for immediate Work at Hand activation.
