---
title: "Stage 2: Scoped Form"
---

Stage 2 Scoped Form defines success criteria before tactics through five sections: objectives (1-3 specific outcomes), deadline (optional), archetype selection (Quick Task, Discovery Mission, etc.), and trait characterization (Urgency, Importance, Complexity, Scale, Support).

Normal pace takes 10 minutes; emergency pace takes 3 minutes with Marvin auto-suggesting based on emergency type.
